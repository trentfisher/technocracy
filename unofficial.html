A few words about being``unofficial''

After almost 3 years of being the official web pages for Technocracy, Inc., CHQ has started up their own web pages. Their pages will be a more flashy introduction to the organization, while mine will remain an archive of past and present articles.

I call these web pages unofficial since I make no claims to be an official representative of this organization. A common disclaimer used throughout the organization, which applies to these web pages as well, is as follows:

No statement of policy is binding upon this organization unless it is issued from CHQ Technocracy, Inc.

However, almost all of the materials I present here are taken directly from official, CHQ approved, literature. The formatting, indexing and organization of the files is my own, but I have made every effort to faithfully reproduce the content of the material. Any materials which are not CHQ approved should be labeled as such.
