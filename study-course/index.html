Technocracy Study Course

This book contained the background information and the detailed explanation of Technocracy. It was originally distributed as several pamphlets, these were later printed together into a small soft-cover book, and finally into a hard-cover book. The last printing was in 1947.

While there are no authorship credits (except for the introduction in earlier editions, which was credited to Arch Jameson), much of this book is credited to M. King Hubbert.

In the 70's this material was condensed and updated to produce the booklet Technocracy: Technological Social Design
