Trent A. Fisher

Trent is the Maintainer of the Technocracy web pages, and kind of de-facto coordinator for many of the organization's online activities. He has been a member since 1983, and has been an officer Section 3, Regional Division 12245 (Portland, OR) off and on throughout that time.

He holds a BS in Computer Science from Portland State University, 1990, and works as a Configuration Management Engineer.
