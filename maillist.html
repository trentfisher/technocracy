Technocracy Mailing List

There is an open mailing list for discussion of Technocracy and various related issues.

To subscribe to it, send a message containing subscribe technocracy to majordomo@pacifier.com. Do NOT put your e-mail address, or anything else on that line; if you do you have to go through an authorization step, which will be confusing, and I know nothing about. Questions about the list can be sent to technocracy-owner@pacifier.com NOT to technocracy@pacifier.com (which goes to everybody on the mailing list).

It is important to note that nothing stated on the mailing list should be taken as representative of Technocracy, even if it is coming from a Technocrat. Technocracy is too important of a concept to be dismissed because of misstatements made on a mailing list. If in doubt, consult Technocracy Inc.'s publications or ask CHQ directly.

There is another mailing list for members of Technocracy, Inc. It is currently a moderated mailing list, to maintain low-volume, high-quality content. To subscribe to it, send a request to trent@technocracy.org. After your membership status has been confirmed by CHQ you will be added to the list.
