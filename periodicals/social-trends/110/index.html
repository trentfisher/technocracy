OUR FASCINATING EARTH: As our population grows.

The continuous growth and spread of the human species has produced numerous environmental disasters throughout the world. The most ominous destruction is that of the tropical forests. These areas are the home of at least half of the earth's species of plants and animals. Yet the destruction of the tropical forests proceeds at a rate equal to ONE FOOTBALL FIELD PER SECOND!!!

by Philip Seff, Ph.D.
