Technocracy -> Periodicals -> Great Lakes Technocrat Search

Published in Cleveland, OH from May/June 1935 to June 1942 with the name 8141. The publishing was moved to Akron, OH under the new name for 3 issues. Publishing ceased for 8 months as it moved to Chicago, IL. In July 1943 publishing resumed in a small digest format, and published bi-monthly until Sep/Oct. 1948.

Here is an index of the issues I have online:

  • Mar/Apr 1944, No. 66 (partial)
  • May/Jun 1947, No. 85 (partial)
