Technocracy Periodicals

This is a list of all the magazines published by Technocracy throughout its history, and a list of the currently published Newsletters. I don't have all of these magazines, so I have had to infer many dates from other magazines of the period. Please send me any corrections or updates.

I have a number of articles from (and many compleat issues of) various magazines available by following the various links. However, even the compleat issues I have are not quite the same as the printed magazines: reprinted articles, some illustrations, filler bits, &c. are not present. Consider subscribing and buying the printed back-issues.

  • The Northwest Technocrat
    Current address:
    2475 Harksell Rd., Ferndale, WA 98248.
    Pubished from early 1939 to the present.
  • Technocracy Digest
    Current address:
    2946 -- 272nd St.
    Aldergrove, B.C. V4W 3R4.
    Published from before May 1935 to the present.
  • Technocracy
    (a.k.a. the `A series') Published by CHQ (which was in New York City at the time) from May 1935 to Dec 1942
  • The Technocrat
    Published in the Los Angeles area (Hollywood, Long Beach, &c.) From the winter of 1934 to 1979.
  • Great Lakes Technocrat
    Published in Akron, OH and, later, Chicago, IL. from July 1942 to Sep/Oct. 1948.
  • Technocratic Trendevents
    Published in Los Angeles, CA. from July 1950 to at least August 1975. (The former date is a extrapolation based on the 1968 issue I have numbered 212, and the latter date is the last one I have, there may have been more.)
  • The Monad
    Published in Kansas City, MO. from before May 1935 to late 1936.
  • Eighty-One Forty-One
    Published in Cleveland, OH. from May/June 1935 to June 1942 when it was renamed Great Lakes Technocrat
  • The Section Post
    Published in Portland, OR. from July 1936 to Early 1939 when it was renamed The Northwest Technocrat
  • Technocratic America
    Published in Colton and later Fontana, CA. from Oct. 1936 to May 1949.
  • The Northern Technocrat
    Published in Edmonton, Alberta from Mid 1936 to early 1941.
  • The Foothills Technocrat
    Published in Calgary, Alberta from late 1936 to early 1941
  • Streamline Age
    Published in Phoenix, AZ. from late 1936 to Fall 1938
  • The Southwest Corner
    Published in San Diego, CA. from late 1936 to late 1940.
  • The Desert Salute
    Published in Hinkley, CA. from late 1936 to 1942.
  • Ninety-Seven Forty-Nine
    Published in Winnipeg, Manitoba from late 1937 to July 1938 when it was renamed The Prarie Technocrat.
  • The Prarie Technocrat
    Published in Winnipeg, Manitoba from July 1938 to late 1941.
