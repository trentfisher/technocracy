This magazine started in July 1936 as The Section Post, published by 12245-1 (Portland, OR). It was renamed The Northwest Technocrat in early 1939. The publishing was moved to Seattle, WA in 1942.

Current Address:
2475 Harksell Rd., Ferndale, WA 98248. Subscriptions to both this and Technocracy Digest are $12.00/year. Sample copies are available.

The Editor has started supplying me with the word processor files used to print the magazine. I used a home-brew converter to turn them into html. Formatting mistakes are most likely mine.
