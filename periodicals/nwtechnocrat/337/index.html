The Northwest Technocrat, 4th quarter 1994, No. 337, Vol. 56

In the passing of the old and the instituting of the new, human conflict will become the bitterest in history. Science is hated and feared by ecclesiastical institutionalism, by corporate enterprise and political parties. The fear and hatred that will be directed by these against science and its technological application will be far greater than those the Inquisition hurled against all the accumulated heresies. Only science and its technological application to the means whereby we live can produce for mankind the control of our social destiny here on this earth. -- Howard Scott
