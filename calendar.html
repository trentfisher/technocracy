Technocracy Events Calendar

Since there are people all over the continent reading this, I thought a calendar of the various events would be useful. This list is in reverse chronological order, so start at the top, and you will find past events at the bottom. If you know of anything that should be listed here, or have any questions, let trent@technocracy.org know.

Last modified 2 Nov 97 by trent

Third Tuesday of each month
Public meeting, 6:00pm to 8:00pm at Campus Ministry at Portland State University (corner of Broadway and Montgomery) Portland, Oregon. Here is a map. Contact Trent for further info. Starting in October, we will be studying the book Geodestinies.
Every Monday
Discussion group, 2:00pm to 4:00pm, at Taylor Library, 2015 Third St., Cuyahoga Falls, OH.
Third Thursday of each month
Public meeting, 5:30pm, at City Island Library, Daytona Beach, Florida. Contact Steve Doll for more information.
Third Sunday of each month
Public Lecture by various speakers. Dinner at 5:30pm, lecture follows. 2946 272nd St., Aldergrove, B.C. Call (604) 856-1535 for further information.
Last Saturday of each month
Current events class at 7:00pm and a dinner preceding at 5:30pm. 2946 272nd St., Aldergrove, B.C. Call (604) 856-1535 for further information.
4 to 6-Sep-99
Annual Labour Day event. At 2946 272nd St., Aldergrove, B.C. There are a number of meetings and meals all three days. Call (604) 856-1535 for further information.
