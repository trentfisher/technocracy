Technocracy


 Welcome to the unofficial home page for Technocracy, Inc. The offical pages are at www.technocracyinc.org. Technocracy, Inc. is a non-profit, non-political, non-sectarian membership organization that was founded in 1933 to research and educate the public about an alternative way of organizing society around scientific and engineering methodologies to alleviate the economic, ecological and social problems that the current system perpetuates.
Technocracy [...] may be concisely described as a technological approach to, and an analysis of, all social phenomena. -- Howard Scott (from the The Hotel Pierre Address)
Essentially Technocracy is a soundly scientific effort to restate economics on a purely physical basis -- H.G. Wells

There is a page of starting points to help you find a good article (according to your particular interests) to find out what Technocracy is about. Look below for more.

Beware of imitations! There are other web pages around that are using the word improperly. Technocracy is not government bureaucrats who use technology, nor is it a technological police state, nor is it misusing technology to further corporate profits, nor is it a shadowy villian in a role-playing game. If you are laboring under such a misapprehension, I urge you to find out what we are really about. These pages represent the works of the original Technocrats.
